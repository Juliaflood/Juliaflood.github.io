The Blarney Stone

This infamous stone is located in Cork Ireland many flock to this stone to kiss the stone for good luck. When the tourists make it up to the top of the castel the Blarney Stone is located on the outside of the grandiouse castle and a guide helps the tourists to hold onto a bar and bend backwards to kiss the stone for good luck.

Ring of Kerry

This vast area of land called the Ring of Kerry is full of very narrow roads that overlook the beatiful bodies of water and mountains that lie on the outskirts of Ireland. The Ring of Kerry shows much of the countryside of Ireland and cliffs.
