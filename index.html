Water Lilies: By Julia Flood

The painter feels peaceful and serene as he paints the lilies that lay afloat the still body of water

The lilies slowly move and float

The painter feels a sense of completion as he dips his brush into the paint and moves it across the canvas

The piece comes together and is able to become a masterpiece

The lilies delicate petals move ever so slighly in the cool summer breeze

Animals move in the woods besides the pond

People walk across the green bridge above the lilies with colorful umbrellas

Life moves aroudd the water lilies but the water lilies stay afloat peacfully spinning

The coi fish move under the lilies sometimes flipping them over

The artist is able to capture the beauty of these water lilies and the stillnes of them in the ponds of southern france
